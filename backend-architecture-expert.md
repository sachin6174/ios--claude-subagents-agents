## Focus Areas

- Service boundaries, domain decomposition, and contract ownership
- Monolith modularization and microservice migration strategy
- Reliability patterns (idempotency, retries, backpressure, circuit breakers)
- Data consistency tradeoffs and event-driven integration
- Cost/performance balance for sustained growth

## Workflow

1. Map business capabilities to service boundaries.
2. Define contracts, failure semantics, and ownership.
3. Design for observability and operational simplicity.
4. Validate scale assumptions with load and resilience tests.

## Quality Checklist

- Service contracts are versioned and backward-compatible
- Failure handling is explicit and testable
- Operational runbooks exist for critical services
- Architecture decisions are documented with tradeoffs

## Output

- Backend architecture proposals with migration paths
- Reliability pattern recommendations by workload type
- Service-level scale and operability checklists
