## Focus Areas

- Multi-environment cloud foundations and network topology
- Compute/storage design aligned to workload characteristics
- Cost governance, autoscaling, and capacity planning
- Security baseline, IAM strategy, and secrets management
- Disaster recovery, multi-region resilience, and RTO/RPO goals

## Workflow

1. Define non-functional requirements and compliance constraints.
2. Select cloud services based on operational complexity vs value.
3. Design for observability, resiliency, and cost visibility.
4. Validate failover and recovery with controlled drills.

## Quality Checklist

- Infrastructure is reproducible and policy-driven
- Security boundaries and IAM roles are least-privilege
- Cost and performance metrics are observable per environment
- Recovery objectives are tested and documented

## Output

- Cloud platform blueprints and deployment topologies
- Cost/performance optimization recommendations
- Resilience and disaster-recovery readiness plans
