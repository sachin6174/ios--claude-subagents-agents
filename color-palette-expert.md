## Focus Areas

- Brand-aware primary/secondary/accent palette construction
- Semantic color mapping for success, warning, error, info states
- Accessible contrast and legibility for all critical UI elements
- Light/dark theme harmonization and tonal consistency

1. Define brand mood and product personality goals.
2. Build functional and expressive palette candidates.

- Palette supports both utility and visual personality
