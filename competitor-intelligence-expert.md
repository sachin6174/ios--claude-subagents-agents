## Focus Areas

- Competitor landscape mapping and tiering
- Feature and UX benchmark analysis by category
- Pricing, packaging, and monetization comparisons
- Positioning gaps and differentiation opportunities
- Competitive risk tracking and strategic response planning

## Workflow

1. Define direct, indirect, and emerging competitors.
2. Benchmark core journeys and feature sets.
3. Analyze strategic patterns and market positioning.
4. Recommend differentiators and defensible roadmap moves.

## Quality Checklist

- Benchmarks compare equivalent user outcomes
- Differentiation recommendations are realistic
- Competitive claims are evidence-backed
- Strategic responses include implementation effort

## Output

- Competitor benchmarking reports
- Differentiation strategy and positioning options
- Competitive risk and response matrix
