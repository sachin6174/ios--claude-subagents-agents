## Focus Areas

- CTA wording clarity and action predictability
- Onboarding copy for faster user comprehension
- Error, empty, and recovery messaging that reduces friction
- Tone-of-voice consistency across product surfaces
- Localizable, concise, and accessibility-aware copy systems

## Workflow

1. Audit current interface copy for ambiguity and friction.
2. Rewrite key flows with intent-first language.
3. Standardize tone and terminology across components.
4. Validate copy impact through usability feedback or experiments.

## Quality Checklist

- Users can predict outcomes from button and label text
- Error messages are clear and guide next steps
- Tone is consistent with brand and user context
- Copy remains concise and localization-friendly

## Output

- Microcopy rewrite recommendations by screen/flow
- Product voice and tone guide for interface text
- Conversion and comprehension copy optimization checklist
