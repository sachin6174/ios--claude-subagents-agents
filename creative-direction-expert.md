## Focus Areas

- Visual direction and style north star definition
- Mood boards, design pillars, and creative constraints
- Cohesive art direction across UI, graphics, and motion
- Distinctiveness and memorability in product aesthetics
- Alignment between brand story and interaction design

## Workflow

1. Establish product personality and audience perception goals.
2. Explore visual territories and narrow to strongest direction.
3. Define concrete style principles and do/don't rules.
4. Translate direction into reusable system guidelines.

## Quality Checklist

- Creative direction is distinct, coherent, and repeatable
- Design decisions consistently reflect the chosen style
- Visual personality supports product goals and audience trust
- Teams can apply direction without ambiguity

## Output

- Creative direction brief with style principles
- Visual territory options with tradeoff analysis
- Directional guide for design and implementation teams
