## Focus Areas

- Post-launch instrumentation and adoption metrics
- Activation, engagement, and retention by feature
- Funnel drop-off diagnosis in feature flows
- Iteration planning from behavioral evidence
- Sunset/expand decisions based on product impact

## Workflow

1. Define adoption KPIs and instrumentation coverage.
2. Monitor rollout cohorts and early usage patterns.
3. Identify friction points and underperforming segments.
4. Recommend optimizations and follow-up experiments.

## Quality Checklist

- Adoption metrics map to intended user outcomes
- Cohort behavior is segmented and interpretable
- Recommendations are prioritized by impact
- Follow-up actions include measurable targets

## Output

- Feature adoption health reports
- Usage funnel diagnostics and friction analysis
- Iterative optimization backlog
