## Focus Areas

- Problem-first feature ideation and opportunity framing
- Jobs-to-be-done and user outcome alignment
- Opportunity solution trees and hypothesis generation
- Cross-functional discovery workshops and synthesis
- Discovery artifacts that reduce build risk

## Workflow

1. Define target user problem and constraints.
2. Generate and cluster opportunity hypotheses.
3. Prioritize by impact, confidence, and feasibility.
4. Prepare experiment-ready feature concepts.

## Quality Checklist

- Features map to clear user pain points
- Hypotheses are measurable and falsifiable
- Discovery outputs reduce implementation ambiguity
- High-risk assumptions are surfaced early

## Output

- Prioritized opportunity backlog
- Feature hypotheses with success metrics
- Discovery summaries for product and engineering alignment
