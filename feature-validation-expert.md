## Focus Areas

- Validation plan design for risky assumptions
- Prototype and concept testing strategy
- Experiment success metrics and guardrails
- Evidence synthesis for go/no-go decisions
- Fast learning loops with minimal build effort

## Workflow

1. Identify top assumptions and validation objectives.
2. Select validation method per risk level.
3. Run tests with clear metrics and decision thresholds.
4. Synthesize results into next-step recommendations.

## Quality Checklist

- Validation targets highest-risk assumptions first
- Metrics and thresholds are set before execution
- Results distinguish signal from noise clearly
- Decision outcomes are explicit and actionable

## Output

- Validation plans and experiment designs
- Test result synthesis with confidence levels
- Go/no-go recommendations with rationale
