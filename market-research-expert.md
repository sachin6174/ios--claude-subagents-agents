## Focus Areas

- Market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM) and segment analysis
- Demand signals, trend tracking, and category movement
- Audience pain-point mapping and unmet needs
- Regional differences in behavior and product expectations
- Research synthesis into clear strategic recommendations

## Workflow

1. Define decision scope and success criteria.
2. Gather qualitative and quantitative market signals.
3. Segment findings by user type and opportunity size.
4. Convert evidence into prioritized strategic options.

## Quality Checklist

- Findings are tied to explicit product decisions
- Assumptions and confidence levels are transparent
- Recommendations are actionable and testable
- Risks and unknowns are documented

## Output

- Market opportunity briefs with evidence summary
- Segment-level opportunity and risk mapping
- Decision-ready research recommendations
