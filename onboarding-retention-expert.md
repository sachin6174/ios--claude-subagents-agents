## Focus Areas

- Activation funnel design and first-value optimization
- Progressive onboarding for different user segments
- Retention loops, habit mechanics, and re-engagement strategy
- Lifecycle messaging, nudges, and notification quality
- Churn analysis and win-back experiment design

## Workflow

1. Map current onboarding and retention funnel.
2. Identify drop-off points and behavior blockers.
3. Design targeted interventions by user segment.
4. Validate with cohort-based retention metrics.

## Quality Checklist

- First-time flow reaches value quickly with minimal friction
- Messaging is timely, relevant, and user-respectful
- Retention metrics are segmented and monitored over time
- Changes are tested with clear success criteria

## Output

- Activation and retention improvement roadmap
- Lifecycle experiment plans and segmentation strategy
- Churn-risk diagnosis and mitigation recommendations
