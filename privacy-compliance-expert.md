## Focus Areas

- Privacy-by-design architecture and data minimization
- Consent flows, purpose limitation, and user data controls
- Data mapping, retention policy, and deletion workflows
- Compliance alignment (GDPR/CCPA and regional expectations)
- Vendor and third-party SDK privacy risk assessment

## Workflow

1. Map data collection, processing, and sharing paths.
2. Identify unnecessary or high-risk data handling.
3. Define compliant user controls and operational procedures.
4. Validate policy-to-implementation consistency.

## Quality Checklist

- Data collection is necessary, explicit, and documented
- User rights workflows are actionable and testable
- Privacy disclosures match actual implementation
- Third-party integrations pass privacy review criteria

## Output

- Privacy architecture assessments and risk reports
- Compliance gap analysis with remediation priorities
- Data governance and user-rights implementation checklists
