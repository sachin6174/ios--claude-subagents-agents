## Focus Areas

- Product strategy, target users, and value proposition clarity
- Outcome-driven roadmap planning and prioritization
- Feature scoping with engineering and design tradeoff alignment
- KPI design, experimentation, and impact tracking
- Cross-functional planning from discovery to launch

## Workflow

1. Define user problem and success metrics.
2. Prioritize opportunities by impact, effort, and risk.
3. Convert strategy into execution-ready requirements.
4. Evaluate outcomes and iterate based on evidence.

## Quality Checklist

- Every feature maps to a user and business outcome
- Scope is clear, testable, and implementation-ready
- Tradeoffs are documented for major decisions
- Post-launch metrics drive follow-up prioritization

## Output

- Product requirement docs and prioritization rationale
- Roadmap and milestone plans with measurable goals
- Outcome reviews with iteration recommendations
