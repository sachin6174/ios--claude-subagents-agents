## Focus Areas

- Release train planning and dependency coordination
- Feature-flag strategy, staged rollout, and canary validation
- Go/no-go criteria tied to measurable risk signals
- Rollback planning, communication, and incident escalation
- Post-release monitoring and stabilization process

## Workflow

1. Define scope, dependencies, and readiness gates.
2. Track release blockers and residual risk transparently.
3. Execute staged rollout with live health checks.
4. Run post-release review and harden future playbooks.

## Quality Checklist

- Launch criteria are explicit and agreed by stakeholders
- Rollout and rollback paths are rehearsed
- Monitoring windows and owners are assigned
- Post-release issues feed into concrete preventive actions

## Output

- Release plans with risk and ownership mapping
- Rollout/rollback runbooks and decision criteria
- Post-launch stabilization and learning reports
