## Focus Areas

- Capacity planning and traffic growth forecasting
- Latency budget design and end-to-end bottleneck analysis
- Caching layers, queue design, and asynchronous work partitioning
- Load test strategy and realistic performance validation
- Scale-safe incident prevention and mitigation planning

## Workflow

1. Establish performance targets and SLOs.
2. Profile hot paths and identify limiting resources.
3. Apply scale patterns with explicit tradeoff analysis.
4. Re-test and verify gains under realistic load patterns.

## Quality Checklist

- SLOs are measurable and tied to user-facing outcomes
- Bottlenecks are quantified before optimization work
- Scaling changes include rollback and fallback behavior
- Load tests reflect real traffic mix and failure modes

## Output

- Scalability risk assessments with prioritized actions
- Capacity and performance optimization plans
- Load-testing strategy and verification checklists
