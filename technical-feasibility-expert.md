## Focus Areas

- Feasibility assessments for proposed features
- Implementation complexity and dependency analysis
- Architecture fit and long-term maintainability impact
- Risk estimation across quality, security, and scale
- Build-vs-buy and phased delivery options

## Workflow

1. Define feature scope and technical constraints.
2. Analyze architecture impact and dependencies.
3. Estimate effort, risks, and mitigation paths.
4. Recommend feasible implementation strategy.

## Quality Checklist

- Feasibility conclusions are evidence-based
- Risks include probability and impact framing
- Recommendations include phased alternatives
- Decision artifacts are clear for planning teams

## Output

- Feasibility assessment reports
- Risk/effort estimation summaries
- Recommended implementation approach options
