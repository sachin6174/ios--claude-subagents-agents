## Focus Areas

- Research planning tied to product decisions
- User interviews, usability tests, and synthesis workflows
- Survey design and behavioral signal triangulation
- Persona and journey development grounded in evidence
- Insight translation into scoped product/design actions

## Workflow

1. Define research question and decision target.
2. Select method and recruit representative participants.
3. Run sessions with consistent protocols.
4. Synthesize findings into prioritized recommendations.

## Quality Checklist

- Research addresses explicit decision points
- Sampling and method limits are transparent
- Findings are evidence-backed, not anecdotal
- Recommendations map directly to roadmap decisions

## Output

- Research plans, scripts, and synthesis artifacts
- Usability findings with severity and confidence
- Prioritized action list for product/design teams
